# Rejected Retrospectives

This directory stores retrospective notes that were considered and declined for promotion. Notes are retained per the "do not delete" principle — they document the reasoning behind a rejection so the same lesson is not re-proposed without new evidence.

## What Belongs Here

- A retrospective where the triage decision was `Rejected` (not `Deferred`).
- The note's `Status` field must read `Rejected`.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Notes that were `Deferred` — those live in `../deferred/`.
- Notes that were promoted — those live in `../promoted/`.
- Notes that have not been triaged — those live in `../inbox/`.
